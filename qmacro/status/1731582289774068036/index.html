TIL about the amazing facility that #jq has for array subtraction. I used it for #AdventOfCode Day 3 (see https://github.com/qmacro/aoc/blob/main/2023/day-3/solution.jq#L65-L75).
Here's a quick example:

; jq -cn '[[1,2],[3,4],[5,6],[0,1]] - [[7,8],[3,4],[0,0]]'
[[1,2],[5,6],[0,1]]
